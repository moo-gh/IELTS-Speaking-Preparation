#### 1. Did you enjoy travelling by car when you were a kid?
**Yes**, **mostly**—**family** **trips** to **relatives** or **mountain** **towns** **felt** like **adventures** with **snacks**, **music**, and **stopping** for **photos**. **Long** **drives** in **summer** **heat** **without** **proper** **AC** **tested** **patience**, but **overall** **car** **travel** **meant** **freedom** **beyond** **the** **neighbourhood**.

---
#### 2. What types of cars do you like?
**Compact** **hatchbacks** or **small** **SUVs**—**easy** to **park**, **fuel**-**efficient**, and **enough** **boot** **space** for **bags** and **groceries**. **Huge** **luxury** **sedans** **look** **impressive** but **feel** **awkward** in **narrow** **city** **streets** where I **actually** **drive**.

---
#### 3. Do you prefer to be a driver or a passenger?
**Driver**, **when** I **know** the **route**—I **like** **control** over **timing**, **music**, and **stops**. As a **passenger**, I **relax** on **highways** or **unfamiliar** **roads**, **especially** at **night** when **concentration** **drains** me **faster** than I **admit**.

---
#### 4. What do you usually do when there is a traffic jam?
**Podcasts**, **short** **language** **drills**, or **checking** **messages** **only** when **fully** **stopped**—**never** **scrolling** while **creeping** **forward**. If **gridlock** **lasts** **ages**, I **switch** **routes** on **maps** or **call** ahead to **warn** **whoever** is **waiting**.

---
#### 5. Do you think car colours are important?
**A** **little**—**dark** **colours** **hide** **dust** **better** in **dry** **cities**, while **bright** **ones** **show** **up** **clearer** at **night**. **Resale** **value** and **personal** **taste** **matter** **more** than **status** **symbolism** for **most** **buyers** I **know**.

---
#### 6. Would you like to own a car in the future?
**Probably**, **once** **work** and **budget** **settle**—**public** **transport** **covers** **daily** **commutes** **now**, but a **car** **helps** for **weekend** **trips**, **family** **visits**, and **areas** **poorly** **served** by **buses**. I'd **prefer** **something** **reliable** and **used** over **flashy** **new** **debt**.
