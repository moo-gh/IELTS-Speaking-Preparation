#### 1. What makes you happy?
**Small** **wins** **mostly**—**finishing** a **difficult** **task**, **sun** after **grey** **weeks**, **laughing** with **friends** over **food**, and **hearing** **good** **news** from **family**. **Grand** **luxury** **helps** **briefly**, but **routine** **kindness** **lasts** **longer** for me.

---
#### 2. Is there anything that has made you feel happy lately?
**Yes**—**passing** a **mock** **test** **score** I'd **been** **chasing**, and a **surprise** **call** from a **cousin** I **hadn't** **spoken** to in **months**. **Both** were **modest** **events**, but they **shifted** my **mood** for **days**.

---
#### 3. Do you think people are happy when buying new things?
**Briefly**, **sometimes**—**new** **shoes** or **gadgets** **spark** **excitement**, then **habituation** **kicks** in and **the** **buzz** **fades**. **Buyers** who **chase** **constant** **upgrades** **risk** **confusing** **pleasure** with **lasting** **contentment**.

---
#### 4. Do you think people in your country are generally happy?
**Mixed**—**warm** **family** **ties** and **strong** **community** **moments** **lift** **spirits**, but **economic** **pressure**, **traffic**, and **uncertainty** **weigh** **heavily** **too**. **Public** **smiles** **don't** **always** **reflect** **private** **stress**, **honestly**.

---
#### 5. What did you do to feel happy when you were young?
**Played** **outside**, **shared** **sweets**, **watched** **cartoons**, and **competed** in **silly** **games** with **cousins** at **gatherings**. **Homework** **stress** **existed**, but **responsibilities** were **lighter**, so **small** **treats** **felt** **enormous**.

---
#### 6. What do you think is important for happiness?
**Health**, **enough** **money** for **basics**, **people** you **trust**, and **work** or **study** that **feels** **meaningful** **at** **least** **sometimes**. **Comparison** on **social** **media** **poisons** **happiness** **fast**; **gratitude** and **realistic** **expectations** **help** **more** than **chasing** **perfection**.
