#### 1. Have you ever been part of a sports team?
**Yes**, **briefly**—**school** **football** and **volleyball** **squads** when I was **younger**, **mostly** for **fun** rather than **serious** **competition**. I **wasn't** **star** **material**, but **training** **taught** me to **show** **up** on **time** and **not** **quit** when **weather** **turned** **ugly**.

---
#### 2. Are team sports popular in your culture?
**Very**—**football** **dominates** **TV** and **street** **games**, while **volleyball** and **basketball** **draw** **strong** **school** **leagues**. **National** **matches** **pause** **family** **dinners**; **even** **people** who **rarely** **play** **follow** **scores** **religiously**.

---
#### 3. What are the differences between team sports and individual sports?
**Team** **sports** **need** **coordination**, **trust**, and **shared** **blame** when **plans** **fail**—**no** **hiding** **behind** **solo** **excuses**. **Individual** **sports** like **running** or **swimming** **reward** **personal** **discipline** but **can** **feel** **lonelier** during **long** **training** **blocks**.

---
#### 4. Do you like watching team games? Why?
**Yes**, **especially** **when** **tactics** **matter**—**passing**, **defence**, and **comebacks** **feel** **dramatic** **together**. **Individual** **events** **can** **thrill** **too**, but **crowd** **reactions** in **team** **games** **multiply** the **excitement**.

---
#### 5. Would you like to join a sports team now?
**Maybe** a **casual** **one**—**weekend** **five**-**a**-**side** **football** or a **recreational** **volleyball** **group** **without** **brutal** **schedules**. **Full** **commitment** **conflicts** with **study** **deadlines** **right** **now**, so I'd **start** **light**.

---
#### 6. What skills do team sports teach people?
**Communication**, **patience**, **leadership**, and **handling** **loss** **without** **blaming** **teammates**. **You** **learn** that **effort** **beats** **ego**—**passing** the **ball** **sometimes** **wins** **more** than **solo** **heroics**.
