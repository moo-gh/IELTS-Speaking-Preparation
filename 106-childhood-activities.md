#### 1. What are your favourite activities?
**Reading**, **walking**, **occasional** **hiking**, and **language** **practice** **outside** **formal** **classes**. **Social** **meals** with **friends** **count** **too**—**low**-**pressure** **time** **away** from **screens** **recharges** me **better** than **marathon** **gaming** **sessions**.

---
#### 2. What were your favourite activities when you were a child?
**Football** in **alleys**, **cycling** around **the** **block**, **collecting** **cards**, and **building** **absurd** **forts** from **cushions** and **blankets**. **Board** **games** with **cousins** during **holidays** **also** **ranked** **high** when **rain** **trapped** us **indoors**.

---
#### 3. Did you prefer to do activities alone or with a group of people when you were a child?
**Mostly** **with** **friends**—**games** **needed** **teams**, and **silence** **felt** **boring** **quickly**. I **enjoyed** **solo** **reading** and **drawing** **too**, but **outdoor** **play** **almost** **always** **turned** **social** **fast**.

---
#### 4. Are there any differences between the activities you liked when you were a child and those you like now?
**Big** **shift**—**less** **running** **wild**, **more** **planned** **study** and **quiet** **hobbies** **fitting** **adult** **schedules**. **Sports** **became** **occasional**; **books** and **walks** **grew** **steadier**. **Fun** **remains**, but **energy** and **time** **shape** **choices** **differently**.

---
#### 5. What activities did you do with your family as a child?
**Picnics**, **visiting** **relatives**, **short** **trips** to **mountains** or **the** **sea**, and **helping** in the **kitchen** during **big** **meals**. **Parents** **pushed** **homework** **first**, but **weekends** **often** **meant** **markets** or **parks** **together**.

---
#### 6. Do children today have different activities from when you were young?
**Partly**—**more** **screens**, **online** **games**, and **structured** **classes** **fill** **calendars** **now**. **Outdoor** **play** **still** **happens**, but **parents** **worry** **more** about **traffic** and **safety**, so **activities** **look** **more** **supervised** than **my** **free**-**range** **childhood**.
