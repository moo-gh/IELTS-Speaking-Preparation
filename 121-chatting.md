**Yes**, **very** **much**—**light** **talk** over **tea**, **memes**, **study** **stress**, and **weekend** **plans** **keeps** **friendships** **alive**. **Long** **silences** **feel** **awkward** with **close** **friends**; **chatting** **fills** **gaps** **naturally**.

---

**Face**-**to**-**face** when **possible**—**tone** and **expressions** **prevent** **misunderstandings**. **Messaging** **wins** for **quick** **updates**, **sharing** **links**, and **friends** **abroad** in **different** **time** **zones**.

**Last** **weekend** with a **cousin** over **dinner**—**career** **plans**, **family** **stories**, and **old** **school** **memories** **stretched** **past** **midnight**. **Long** **talks** **rarely** **happen** **weekdays** when **everyone** **rushes** **home** **tired**.

---

**Average**—I **can** **start** with **safe** **topics** like **weather**, **traffic**, or **shared** **classes**, but **deep** **chat** **needs** **time**. **Smiling** and **listening** **more** than **talking** **helps** at **first** **meetings**.
