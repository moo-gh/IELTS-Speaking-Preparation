#### 1. Do you like watching sports programs on TV?
**Sometimes**—**major** **tournaments** like **World** **Cup** **football** or **Olympics** **highlights** **pull** me in, but I **rarely** **watch** **full** **seasons** of **league** **matches** **at** **home**. **Short** **recaps** and **analysis** **shows** **suit** me **better** than **three**-**hour** **marathons**.

---
#### 2. Do you like to watch live sports games?
**Yes**, **when** the **atmosphere** **matters**—**local** **derbies**, **national** **team** **games**, or **any** **match** where **crowd** **noise** **adds** **energy**. **Live** **TV** **works** **too**, but **stadiums** **feel** **more** **memorable** if **tickets** **aren't** **absurdly** **priced**.

---
#### 3. Who do you like to watch sports games with?
**Friends** or **cousins** who **actually** **care** about the **score**—**shouting** at the **screen** **together** is **half** the **fun**. **Family** **gatherings** during **big** **events** **also** **work** well when **snacks** and **tea** **flow** **freely**.

---
#### 4. What kinds of games do you expect to watch in the future?
**Football** **mainly**, plus **occasional** **volleyball** and **combat** **sports** **highlights** friends **recommend**. **Esports** **might** **creep** in **too** if **major** **finals** **hit** **mainstream** **channels** **more** **often**.

---
#### 5. Do you prefer to watch sports games alone or with a group of friends?
**With** **friends**, **definitely**—**solo** **viewing** **feels** **flat** unless I'm **studying** **tactics** **seriously**. **Group** **watching** **turns** **boring** **draws** into **social** **evenings** even when **nobody** **scores**.

---
#### 6. Have you ever watched a sports game in a stadium?
**Yes**, **a** **few** **times**—**local** **football** **matches** and **once** a **big** **volleyball** **game** with **school** **friends**. **Noise**, **flags**, and **late** **night** **traffic** **afterwards** **stick** in my **memory** **more** than **exact** **scores**.
