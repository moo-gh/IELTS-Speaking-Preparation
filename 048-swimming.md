#### 1. Do you like swimming?
**Yes**—it's **refreshing** in **hot** **weather** and a **full-body** **workout** that **doesn't** **jar** my **joints** the way **running** **sometimes** does. I'm not **competitive** about it; I **swim** at a **steady** **pace** to **relax** and **clear** my **lungs**, **provided** the **water** is **clean** and not **freezing**.

---
#### 2. Is swimming popular in your country?
**Fairly** **popular**, especially in **summer** when **families** **head** to **pools**, **lidos**, or the **coast**. In **cities**, **indoor** **leisure** **centres** stay **busy** **year-round** with **lane** **swimmers** and **beginners'** **classes**, though not everyone **learned** young, so **confidence** in **deep** **water** **varies**.

---
#### 3. Where do you like to swim?
I **prefer** **supervised** **pools** where **lifeguards** are **on** **duty** and **lanes** are **marked**—it's **predictable** and **safer** for **training** **lengths**. **Open-water** **swimming** looks **appealing** on **calm** **days**, but I'm **wary** of **strong** **currents**, **cold** **shock**, and **visibility**, so I **stick** to **places** I **know**.

---
#### 4. How often do you go swimming?
**On** **average**, maybe **once** or **twice** a **month** when my **schedule** **allows**—**more** in **holiday** **season**, **less** in **winter** unless I **force** myself to **book** a **pool** **session**. I'd **like** to go **weekly**, but **commuting** and **evening** **commitments** often **get** in the **way**.

---
#### 5. Did you learn to swim when you were a child?
**Yes**—**primary** **school** **arranged** **basic** **lessons** at a **local** **pool**, **starting** with **floating** and **kicking** with a **board**. I wasn't **fearless** **straight** **away**, but **repetition** **built** **confidence**, and **by** **my** **teens** I could **manage** **front** **crawl** and **backstroke** without **panicking**.

---
#### 6. What do you think are the benefits of swimming?
**Physically**, it **improves** **stamina** and **muscle** **tone** while being **gentle** on **knees** and **ankles**. **Mentally**, **rhythmic** **breathing** can feel **meditative**—**less** **screen** time, **more** **focus** on **movement**. **Socially**, it's a **skill** that **matters** for **safety** near **water**, not just for **fitness**.
