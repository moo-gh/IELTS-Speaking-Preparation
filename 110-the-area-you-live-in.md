#### 1. Do you live in a noisy or a quiet area?
**Mixed**—**main** **roads** and **markets** **bring** **traffic** and **honking** **until** **late**, but **our** **lane** **itself** **settles** **down** after **midnight**. **Weekends** **get** **louder** with **weddings** and **family** **gatherings** **nearby**.

---
#### 2. Are the people in your neighbourhood nice and friendly?
**Generally**, **yes**—**neighbours** **greet** each other, **borrow** **sugar** or **tools** **occasionally**, and **check** in during **power** **cuts** or **rough** **weather**. **Like** **anywhere**, **a** **few** **families** **keep** **to** **themselves**, but **overall** **tone** is **polite**.

---
#### 3. Do you like the area that you live in?
**Mostly**—**shops**, **buses**, and **a** **small** **park** are **within** **walking** **distance**, which **saves** **time** **daily**. I'd **trade** **some** **noise** for **greener** **streets**, but **convenience** **wins** for **now**.

---
#### 4. Where do you like to go in that area?
**A** **corner** **bakery**, a **quiet** **café**, and a **riverside** **path** where **joggers** and **elderly** **walkers** **share** **the** **same** **breeze**. **Friday** **markets** **feel** **lively** without **needing** a **long** **trip** **across** **town**.

---
#### 5. What are some changes in the area recently?
**More** **cafés**, **tighter** **parking**, **new** **apartment** **blocks**, and **better** **street** **lighting** on **lanes** that **used** to **feel** **unsafe** at **night**. **Older** **shops** **closed** **too**, **replaced** by **phone** **accessory** **stores** and **fast** **food** **chains**.

---
#### 6. Do you know any of your neighbours?
**A** **handful** **well**—**families** on **the** **same** **floor** and **shopkeepers** I **see** **daily**. I **don't** **know** **everyone** **by** **name**, but **faces** are **familiar** enough to **nod** and **ask** **how** **their** **kids** are **doing**.
