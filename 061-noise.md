#### 1. Do you mind noise?
**It** **depends** on **context**—**background** **chatter** in a **café** can feel **cosy**, but **drilling** at **seven** **a.m.** or **motorbikes** **revving** under my **window** **ruin** my **mood** **fast**. I'm **not** **ultra**-**sensitive**, but I **notice** when **volume** **jumps** **without** **warning**.

---
#### 2. What kinds of noise do you come across in daily life?
**Traffic**, **sirens**, **construction**, and the **hum** of **air** **conditioning** in **offices** are the **usual** **suspects**. At **home** it's **neighbours'** **TVs**, **lift** **doors**, and **occasional** **parties** down the **hall**; **outdoors** you **add** **buses**, **street** **musicians**, and **delivery** **scooters** **beeping**.

---
#### 3. Is there too much noise in your city?
**In** **central** **areas**, **honestly** **yes**—**rush** **hour** **feels** like a **constant** **low**-**grade** **roar**, and **roadworks** **seem** **never**-**ending** on **main** **routes**. **Residential** **pockets** are **quieter**, but you **trade** **peace** for **longer** **commutes** if you **live** **far** from **work**.

---
#### 4. How do you feel when you are in a noisy place?
**Initially** I **adapt**, then **fatigue** **creeps** in—my **shoulders** **tighten** and I **start** **missing** **half** of what people **say**. **Concerts** are **fun** **noise**; **crowded** **stations** at **peak** **time** feel **overwhelming** and I **count** **minutes** until I can **escape**.

---
#### 5. Do you prefer a quiet or noisy environment?
**Quiet** for **studying**, **sleeping**, and **serious** **conversations**—I **need** **headspace** to **think** **clearly**. **Mild** **buzz** suits **social** **meals** or **markets** where **silence** would feel **unnatural**; I **like** **balance**, not **extremes** **all** **day**.

---
#### 6. What do you do to reduce noise when studying or working?
**Noise**-**cancelling** **earphones**, **closed** **doors**, and **choosing** **library** **corners** away from **printers** and **vending** **machines**. I **also** **avoid** **facing** **open** **windows** on **busy** **streets** and I **mute** **phone** **alerts** so **pings** don't **shatter** my **focus** **every** **few** **minutes**.
