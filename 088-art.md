#### 1. Do you like art?
**Yes**, **in** **moderation**—**paintings**, **sculpture**, and **street** **murals** **catch** my **eye** when they **tell** a **story** or **use** **colour** **boldly**. I'm **not** an **expert**; I **enjoy** **looking** **without** **pretending** every **abstract** **canvas** **speaks** to me **deeply**.

---
#### 2. Did you study art at school?
**Basic** **classes**—**drawing**, **simple** **perspective**, and **craft** **projects** with **paper** and **paint** in **primary** and **early** **secondary** **school**. **Serious** **technique** **wasn't** **emphasised**; it **felt** **more** like **creative** **breaks** from **maths** than **training** **future** **artists**.

---
#### 3. What kind of art do you like?
**Landscapes**, **portraits** with **honest** **expressions**, and **photography** that **captures** **everyday** **moments** **without** **heavy** **filters**. **Modern** **installations** **work** when **interactive**; **pure** **conceptual** **pieces** **lose** me if the **label** **does** **all** the **work**.

---
#### 4. Have you ever been to an art gallery?
**Yes**—**local** **galleries**, **museum** **wings**, and **temporary** **exhibitions** when **friends** **visit** or **student** **discounts** **apply**. I **walk** **slowly**, **read** **captions**, and **leave** when **feet** **ache** rather than **forcing** **every** **room**.

---
#### 5. Do you think art is important?
**It** **is**—it **preserves** **culture**, **questions** **power**, and **gives** **people** **beauty** when **life** **feels** **grey**. **Funding** **arts** **schools** and **public** **spaces** **matters** even when **budgets** **tighten**; **creativity** **isn't** **luxury** **only** for **wealthy** **cities**.

---
#### 6. Would you like to learn to draw or paint?
**Casually**, **yes**—enough to **sketch** **travel** **scenes** and **relax** **without** **judging** **every** **line**. **Professional** **skill** **would** **take** **years**; I'd **settle** for **enjoyable** **hobby** **progress** on **quiet** **weekends**.
