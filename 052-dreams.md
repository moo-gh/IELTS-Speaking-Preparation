#### 1. Do you often remember your dreams?
**Hit**-**and**-**miss**—some **mornings** I **wake** **up** with a **vivid** **clip** **playing** in my **head**, especially after **stressful** **days**, and other **times** there's just a **blank** **fade**. If I **don't** **jot** **something** **down** **immediately**, the **details** **dissolve** **within** **minutes**, **no** **matter** how **sure** I was I'd **recall** **them** **over** **breakfast**.

---
#### 2. Do you like hearing about other people's dreams?
**In** **moderation**—a **funny** or **bizarre** **story** can be **entertaining** over **coffee**, but a **blow**-**by**-**blow** **account** of **random** **symbols** **risk** **losing** the **listener** **fast**. I **try** to be **polite** because **dreams** feel **personal**, even when the **plot** **doesn't** **quite** **translate**.

---
#### 3. Do you think dreams are important?
**Not** in a **mystical** **forecasting** **sense** for me, but they **probably** **reflect** **mood**, **worries**, and what my **brain** is **processing** **behind** **the** **scenes**. **Therapists** **sometimes** **explore** **recurring** **themes**, which **suggests** they can **carry** **emotional** **weight** even if they aren't **literal** **messages** **from** **beyond**.

---
#### 4. Would you like to know what other people dream about you?
**Curious**, **yes**—**harmlessly**—but I'd **take** it **with** a **pinch** of **salt** because **dream** **logic** **isn't** **reality**. If **someone** **said** they **dreamt** I was **flying** a **bus**, I'd **laugh** **rather** than **read** **too** **much** **into** **my** **character** **from** **that** **alone**.

---
#### 5. Do you think dreams have special meanings?
**Symbolically**, **perhaps**—**themes** like **being** **chased** or **missing** an **exam** are **widely** **shared** and **map** **onto** **anxiety** **fairly** **clearly**. I'm **sceptical** of **cookie**-**cutter** **dream** **dictionaries** **though**; **context** and **culture** **shape** what **feels** **significant**, so **interpretation** **shouldn't** be **overly** **rigid**.

---
#### 6. Have you ever had a dream that stayed with you for a long time?
**A** **couple**—usually **linked** to **big** **life** **changes** when my **sleep** was **patchy** and my **mind** was **overloaded**. The **images** **blurred** **eventually**, but the **atmosphere**—**tension** or **relief**—**lingered** a **while**, **almost** like **emotional** **after**-**glow** **rather** than a **precise** **narrative** I'd **want** to **replay** on **purpose**.
