#### 1. Do you like jewellery?
**In** **moderation**—a **simple** **chain** or **pair** of **earrings** can **finish** an **outfit** **without** **shouting** for **attention**. I'm **not** **drawn** to **heavy** **bling** or **pieces** that **clink** **every** time I **move**; I **prefer** things I **forget** I'm **wearing** until someone **compliments** them.

---
#### 2. Do you often wear jewellery?
**Not** **daily**—maybe **once** or **twice** a **week** for **work** or **evening** **plans**, and **almost** **never** at **home** when I'm **cooking** or **exercising**. **Stud** **earrings** are my **default** because they **don't** **catch** on **scarves** or **headphones** the way **hoops** **sometimes** do.

---
#### 3. What kind of jewellery do you like?
**Understated** **metal**—**silver**-**toned** or **gold**-**plated** **rather** than **chunky** **costume** **sets**. I **like** **thin** **rings** with **clean** **lines** and **occasional** **stones** that **catch** **light** **subtly**; **big** **logo** **pendants** and **layered** **arm** **cuffs** feel **too** **performative** for **me**.

---
#### 4. Have you ever given jewellery as a gift?
**Yes**—I **bought** my **mother** a **modest** **necklace** for her **birthday** after **saving** up from a **part-time** **job**; it **wasn't** **expensive**, but the **box** and **note** **mattered** **more** than the **price** **tag**. I've **also** **helped** **friends** **pick** **earrings** when they **panicked** about **sizes** and **metal** **allergies**.

---
#### 5. Why do some people wear a lot of jewellery?
**Culture**, **celebration**, and **self-expression** **mix** **together**—**weddings** and **festivals** **invite** **bold** **sets**, while **others** **stack** **pieces** as a **fashion** **statement** or **status** **signal**. I **respect** the **craft**; I **just** **personally** **feel** **overloaded** when **every** **finger** and **wrist** is **occupied**.

---
#### 6. Do people in your country like to wear jewellery?
**Many** **do**, **especially** at **formal** **events** and among **older** **generations** who **treat** **gold** as **savings** you can **wear**. **Younger** **city** **crowds** **often** **favour** **minimal** **designs** or **skip** it **altogether**, so you'd **see** **both** **heirloom** **pieces** and **bare** **wrists** on the **same** **street**.
