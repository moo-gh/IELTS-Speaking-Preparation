#### 1. Do you like taking pictures of different views?
**Yes**, **especially** **sunset** **skylines**, **mountain** **ridges**, and **sea** **horizons** when **light** **turns** **soft**. I'm **not** **obsessed** with **gear**—my **phone** **captures** **enough** for **memories** and **sharing** with **friends** **abroad**.

---
#### 2. Do you prefer views in urban areas or rural areas?
**Rural** for **peace**—**valleys**, **fields**, and **quiet** **lakes** **clear** my **head** **faster** than **concrete**. **Urban** **views** **excite** me **too** when **night** **lights** **spread** like **circuits** across **hills**, but **noise** **follows** **the** **photo**.

---
#### 3. Do you prefer views in your own country or in other countries?
**Both** **offer** **something** **different**—**home** **landscapes** **feel** **personal** and **nostalgic**; **abroad** **surprises** you with **shapes** and **colours** you **never** **imagined**. **Travel** **views** **stick** **harder** because **everything** **feels** **new** at **once**.

---
#### 4. Have you seen an unforgettable and beautiful view or scenery?
**Yes**—**mountain** **roads** above **cloud** **lines**, **desert** **sunsets** that **turn** **sand** **gold**, and **coastal** **cliffs** where **wind** **almost** **pushed** us **off** our **feet**. **One** **hilltop** **city** **view** at **night** **still** **pops** into my **head** when I **feel** **stressed**.

---
#### 5. What kind of view relaxes you the most?
**Water** **plus** **open** **sky**—**rivers**, **lakes**, or **calm** **sea** **edges** with **few** **buildings** **blocking** the **horizon**. **Green** **hills** **after** **rain** **rank** **second**; **grey** **office** **windows** **never** **count**.

---
#### 6. Do you enjoy views from tall buildings?
**Yes**, **briefly**—**rooftop** **cafés** and **observation** **decks** **feel** **dramatic**, though **height** **makes** my **stomach** **flutter** if I **lean** **too** **close**. **Photos** from **up** **high** **justify** the **climb** even when **lifts** **take** **forever**.
