#### 1. Do you like mornings?
**Mixed**—**early** **sunlight** and **quiet** **streets** can feel **peaceful**, but **alarm** **clocks** at **six** **still** **feel** **cruel** on **dark** **winter** **days**. I **respect** **morning** **people** more than I **naturally** **am** one.

---
#### 2. What do you usually do in the morning?
**Shower**, **quick** **breakfast**, **check** **messages**, then **commute** or **open** **study** **notes** before my **brain** **fully** **boots**. I **try** to **avoid** **scrolling** **news** **for** **twenty** **minutes**; **some** **days** I **win**, **some** **days** I **don't**.

---
#### 3. Do you think breakfast is important?
**Yes** for **focus**—**skipping** it leaves me **light**-**headed** by **mid**-**morning** and **prone** to **snacking** **badly**. It **doesn't** need to be **fancy**; **toast**, **yoghurt**, or **eggs** **beat** **running** on **coffee** **alone**.

---
#### 4. Are you a morning person or a night person?
**More** of a **night** **person** by **habit**, though I **function** **better** when I **shift** **earlier** for **work** or **exams**. **Late** **nights** **feel** **productive** until **the** **next** **morning** **proves** **otherwise**.

---
#### 5. What did you do in the morning when you were young?
**Parents** **hurried** me through **breakfast**, **school** **bag** **checks**, and **short** **walks** to the **bus** **stop** with **friends**. **Weekends** were **slower**—**cartoons**, **bread** with **jam**, and **playing** **outside** once **homework** **wasn't** **looming**.

---
#### 6. Would you like to change your morning routine?
**Yes**—I'd **like** **consistent** **wake**-**up** **times**, **less** **phone** **use**, and **ten** **minutes** of **stretching** before **screens** **take** **over**. **Small** **changes** **beat** **dramatic** **makeovers** I **abandon** by **Wednesday**.
