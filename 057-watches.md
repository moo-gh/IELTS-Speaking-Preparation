#### 1. Do you wear a watch?
**Occasionally**—I **keep** a **simple** **analogue** **watch** for **interviews** and **travel** days when I **don't** want to **fish** my **phone** out of a **bag** **every** **two** **minutes**. **Day** **to** **day** I **rely** on my **lock** **screen** **clock**, which is **lazy** but **honest**.

---
#### 2. Have you ever received a watch as a gift?
**Yes**—my **parents** gave me a **modest** **digital** **watch** when I **finished** **secondary** **school**; it **felt** **symbolic**, like **"you're** **responsible** **now"** even though I **mostly** **used** it to **time** **exam** **sections**. I **still** **have** it in a **drawer**, **battery** **long** **dead**, **more** **sentiment** than **utility**.

---
#### 3. Do many people in your country wear watches?
**Fewer** than **a** **generation** **ago**, **especially** **under** **thirty**—**phones** **cover** **time**, **alarms**, and **step** **counts** in **one** **device**. You **still** **spot** **classic** **straps** in **offices** and on **older** **relatives** who **treat** a **watch** as **part** of **dressing** **properly**, not a **gadget**.

---
#### 4. Why do some people wear expensive watches?
**Status**, **craftsmanship**, and **tradition** **overlap**—some **buyers** **enjoy** **mechanical** **engineering** and **heritage** **brands**; **others** want a **visible** **signal** at **meetings** that they **can** **afford** **luxury** **without** **saying** it **out** **loud**. I **get** the **appeal** of **precision** **tools**; I'm **less** **sold** on **logo** **flexing**.

---
#### 5. Are you often late?
**I** **try** **not** to be—being **late** **stresses** me because I **picture** people **checking** the **door** and **wondering** if they **misread** the **plan**. **Traffic** and **last-minute** **delays** **still** **trip** me **up** **sometimes**, but I **pad** **travel** **time** when the **appointment** **matters**.

---
#### 6. Do you prefer a watch or your phone to check the time?
**Phone** for **convenience**—it's **already** in my **hand** when **messages** **ping**. A **watch** wins when I want **fewer** **distractions** or I'm **in** a **meeting** **room** where **glancing** at a **screen** **looks** **rude**; then a **quick** **wrist** **flick** feels **more** **polite**.
