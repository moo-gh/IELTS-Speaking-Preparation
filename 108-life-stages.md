#### 1. Do you enjoy being the age you are now?
**Mostly**, **yes**—**more** **freedom** than **childhood**, **clearer** **goals** than **early** **teens**, but **also** **more** **pressure** about **career** and **money**. I **wouldn't** **rewind** **time**, though **occasional** **nostalgia** for **simpler** **school** **days** **hits** **hard**.

---
#### 2. What did you often do with your friends in your childhood?
**Played** **outside**, **swapped** **comics**, **rode** **bikes**, **argued** over **football** **teams**, and **built** **ridiculous** **plans** for **secret** **clubs**. **Rain** **moved** us **indoors** for **board** **games** and **endless** **snacks** **parents** **eventually** **limited**.

---
#### 3. What do you think is the most important at the moment?
**Building** **skills** that **last**—**language**, **discipline**, **health** **habits**, and **relationships** that **survive** **busy** **seasons**. **Short**-**term** **grades** **matter**, but **character** and **adaptability** **feel** **more** **valuable** **long** **term**.

---
#### 4. Do you have any plans for the next five years?
**Finish** **studies**, **gain** **solid** **work** **experience**, **improve** **English** to a **professional** **level**, and **travel** **a** **bit** if **budget** **allows**. **Exact** **job** **titles** **shift**, but **staying** **curious** and **financially** **stable** are **fixed** **targets**.

---
#### 5. How do people remember each stage of their lives?
**Through** **photos**, **stories** **relatives** **repeat**, **school** **records**, and **songs** that **transport** you **back** **instantly**. **Milestones** like **graduations**, **first** **jobs**, and **moves** **mark** **chapters** **more** than **random** **weekdays** **blur** **together**.

---
#### 6. At what age do you think people are the happiest?
**No** **single** **age**—**children** **enjoy** **carefree** **play**, **young** **adults** **chase** **dreams**, **parents** **find** **joy** in **family**, and **older** **people** **often** **value** **peace** **more**. **Happiness** **depends** on **health**, **support**, and **expectations**, not **birthday** **numbers** **alone**.
