#### 1. Are there many tall buildings where you live?
**In** **the** **city** **centre**, **yes**—**apartment** **towers**, **office** **blocks**, and **new** **shopping** **complexes** **cluster** near **main** **roads**. **Older** **neighbourhoods** stay **lower** with **narrow** **streets** and **flat** **roofs**; **height** **signals** **money** and **density** **more** than **everywhere** **looks** **like** a **skyline**.

---
#### 2. Do you like modern or traditional buildings?
**Both**, **for** **different** **reasons**—**traditional** **courtyards**, **brick** **work**, and **domed** **roofs** **feel** **rooted** in **history**; **clean** **modern** **design** **suits** **offices** and **museums** when **light** and **space** **matter**. **Ugly** **concrete** **blocks** from **rushed** **eras** are the **type** I **wish** **cities** **would** **replace** **sooner**.

---
#### 3. Do you take photos of buildings?
**Sometimes**—**unusual** **facades**, **reflections** in **glass**, or **old** **mosques** and **bazaars** when **light** **hits** **just** **right**. I'm **not** an **architecture** **tourist** **every** **weekend**, but **travel** **albums** **always** **include** **a** **few** **building** **shots**.

---
#### 4. Is there a building you would like to visit?
**Several** **abroad**—**historic** **cathedrals**, **famous** **museums**, and **landmark** **towers** I've **seen** **only** in **documentaries**. **Locally**, **restored** **palaces** and **modern** **cultural** **centres** **stay** on my **list** when **friends** **visit** and **want** a **proper** **city** **walk**.

---
#### 5. Would you like to live in a tall building?
**Maybe** **mid**-**rise**, **not** **top** **floor** **penthouse** **life**—**views** **appeal**, but **lifts**, **wind**, and **emergency** **stairs** **matter** if **power** **cuts** **happen**. **Lower** **floors** with **balcony** **plants** **balance** **convenience** and **less** **vertigo** for me.

---
#### 6. What kinds of buildings are common in your country?
**Mixed**—**historic** **religious** **sites**, **bazaar** **rows**, **concrete** **apartments** from **recent** **decades**, and **glass** **towers** in **business** **districts**. **Villages** **favour** **courtyard** **homes**; **coastal** **cities** **blend** **old** **ports** with **new** **hotels** and **resorts**.
