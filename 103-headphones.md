#### 1. Do you use headphones?
**Yes**, **almost** **daily**—**podcasts** on **commutes**, **music** while **studying**, and **calls** when **shared** **spaces** **get** **noisy**. I **keep** a **pair** in my **bag** so **sudden** **library** **crowds** **don't** **ruin** **concentration**.

---
#### 2. What type of headphones do you use?
**Over**-**ear** **ones** at **home** for **comfort** and **better** **sound**; **small** **wireless** **earbuds** for **walking** and **gym** **sessions**. **Cheap** **wired** **earphones** **stay** as **backup** when **battery** **dies** at the **worst** **moment**.

---
#### 3. When would you use headphones?
**Studying**, **commuting**, **exercise**, **late**-**night** **videos** when **family** is **sleeping**, and **online** **classes** in **noisy** **cafés**. **Language** **listening** **practice** **also** **depends** on them so I **hear** **clear** **pronunciation** **without** **disturbing** **others**.

---
#### 4. In what conditions would you not use headphones?
**Crossing** **busy** **roads**, **talking** to **shop** **staff**, **family** **meals**, and **any** **situation** where **awareness** **matters** **more** than **entertainment**. I **also** **skip** them when **ears** **feel** **sore** after **long** **sessions**—**rest** **prevents** **headaches**.

---
#### 5. How long do you wear headphones each day?
**One** to **three** **hours** on **average**, **more** during **exam** **seasons** or **long** **travel** **days**. I **take** **breaks** **every** **hour** to **let** **ears** **breathe** and **check** **volume** **isn't** **creeping** **too** **high**.

---
#### 6. Do you prefer wireless or wired headphones?
**Wireless** for **convenience**—**no** **tangled** **cables** in **pockets**—but **wired** **sounds** **fine** and **never** **dies** **mid**-**song**. **Price** **matters** **too**; **mid**-**range** **wireless** **earbuds** **hit** the **sweet** **spot** for **daily** **use**.
