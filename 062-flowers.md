#### 1. Do you like flowers?
**Yes**, **in** **small** **doses**—a **vase** on a **windowsill** or **wildflowers** on a **walk** **lifts** a **room** **without** **much** **effort**. I'm **not** **obsessed** with **Latin** **names** or **arranging** **bouquets**, but I **notice** **colour** and **scent** when **seasons** **change**.

---
#### 2. What flowers do you like?
**Simple** **ones**—**daisies**, **lavender**, **tulips** in **spring**, and **roses** when they **smell** **real** rather than **like** **air** **freshener**. **Overly** **elaborate** **displays** feel **wasteful** to me; I **prefer** **a** **few** **stems** that **last** **well** in **water**.

---
#### 3. Are flowers popular in your country?
**Very**—**markets** **sell** **bundles** for **weddings**, **funerals**, and **Nowruz**-**style** **spring** **tables** in many **homes**. **Balconies** in **cities** **overflow** with **pots**; **rural** **areas** have **fruit** **blossom** that **feels** like **free** **decoration** **every** **year**.

---
#### 4. Have you ever grown flowers?
**A** **few** **pots** on a **balcony**—**basil** **mixed** with **marigolds** because I'd **read** they **deter** **pests**, though **honestly** the **basil** **outlasted** the **flowers**. As a **child** I **planted** **sunflower** **seeds** at **school** and **felt** **ridiculously** **proud** when they **towered** over me.

---
#### 5. When do people give flowers as gifts?
**Birthdays**, **graduations**, **romantic** **dates**, and **hospital** **visits** are the **obvious** **ones**; **apologies** **sometimes** **arrive** as **roses** **too**. **Funerals** use **white** **lilies** or **chrysanthemums** depending on **tradition**—**flowers** **carry** **emotion** when **words** **fall** **short**.

---
#### 6. Would you like to learn more about flowers?
**Casually**, **yes**—enough to **know** what **thrives** in **shade** versus **sun** and which **plants** are **safe** around **pets**. I **don't** plan to **become** a **florist**, but **basic** **gardening** **knowledge** would **stop** me **killing** **every** **gift** **bouquet** **within** **a** **week**.
