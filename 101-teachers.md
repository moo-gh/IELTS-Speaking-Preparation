#### 1. Do you have a favourite teacher?
**Yes**—an **English** **teacher** who **pushed** **discussion** **beyond** **textbook** **answers** and **corrected** **pronunciation** **without** **embarrassing** **students**. **Math** **teachers** who **explained** **why** a **formula** **worked** **also** **rank** **high** in my **memory**.

---
#### 2. Do you want to be a teacher in the future?
**Not** **full**-**time**, **honestly**—I **respect** the **patience** and **energy** it **demands**, but my **plans** **lean** toward **other** **fields**. **Occasional** **tutoring** or **workshop** **teaching** **appeals** **more** than **running** a **classroom** **year** **after** **year**.

---
#### 3. Do you have a teacher from your past that you still remember?
**Several**—**primary** **school** **teachers** who **made** **story** **time** **magical**, and a **secondary** **school** **history** **teacher** who **turned** **dates** into **real** **dramas** instead of **dry** **lists**. **Their** **voices** **pop** **back** when I **hear** **certain** **topics** **mentioned**.

---
#### 4. Are you still in touch with your primary school teachers?
**No**, **not** **directly**—**social** **media** **might** **connect** **some** **classmates** to **them**, but I **haven't** **sent** **messages** **myself**. **School** **reunions** **sometimes** **bring** **news** **second**-**hand** about **who** **retired** or **moved** **abroad**.

---
#### 5. In what way has your favourite teacher helped you?
**Confidence** **mainly**—**encouraging** me to **speak** **up**, **giving** **honest** **feedback** on **essays**, and **showing** that **mistakes** were **normal** **steps**, not **shameful** **failures**. That **attitude** **still** **helps** when I **present** or **debate** **now**.

---
#### 6. What makes a good teacher?
**Clear** **explanations**, **fair** **rules**, **genuine** **interest** in **students**, and **enough** **humour** to **keep** **heavy** **topics** **bearable**. **Strict** **without** being **cruel** **works** **best**—**fear** **might** **quiet** a **room**, but **respect** **keeps** **people** **learning** **longer**.
