#### 1. Do you like shoes?
**Practically** **yes**—**comfort** **matters** **more** than **fashion** **labels** for me. A **decent** **pair** can **save** your **feet** on **long** **walks** or **commutes**, but I **don't** **collect** **dozens** of **styles** **just** to **match** **outfits**.

---
#### 2. How often do you buy shoes?
**Once** or **twice** a **year**, **usually** when **soles** **wear** **thin** or **rain** **seeps** through **old** **trainers**. **Impulse** **buys** are **rare**; I **try** them **on** **properly** and **walk** around the **shop** before **paying**.

---
#### 3. What kind of shoes do you prefer?
**Sneakers** for **daily** **life** and **light** **walking**, **boots** in **winter** when **pavements** are **wet**, and **simple** **formal** **shoes** **only** for **interviews** or **weddings**. **High** **heels** aren't **for** me—**balance** and **blisters** **win** the **argument**.

---
#### 4. Have you ever bought shoes online?
**Yes**, **but** **carefully**—I **check** **size** **charts**, **read** **reviews** about **width**, and **prefer** **brands** I **already** **know**. **Returns** are **fine** when **fit** is **wrong**, but **walking** **in** **ill**-**fitting** **shoes** for **weeks** because I **was** **lazy** **teaches** you to **measure** **twice**.

---
#### 5. Do you think expensive shoes are better?
**Not** **always**—**price** **sometimes** **buys** **better** **materials** and **craftsmanship**, but **marketing** **inflates** **costs** **too**. A **mid**-**range** **trainer** with **good** **cushioning** **beats** a **flashy** **logo** that **hurts** after **an** **hour**.

---
#### 6. Is it important to wear comfortable shoes?
**Absolutely**—**sore** **feet** **ruin** **mood**, **posture**, and **productivity** **faster** than **people** **admit**. I'd **rather** **look** **plain** and **walk** **pain**-**free** than **suffer** in **tight** **shoes** **all** **day** for **a** **photo**.
