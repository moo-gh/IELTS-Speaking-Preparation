#### 1. Do you like bags?
**Practically** **yes**—a **decent** **bag** **saves** my **shoulders** and **stops** me **juggling** **keys**, **wallet**, and **charger** in **coat** **pockets**. I'm **not** **collecting** **designer** **labels**; I **care** about **comfort**, **zips** that **don't** **jam**, and **pockets** I can **find** **blind**.

---
#### 2. What kind of bags do you like?
**Simple** **backpacks** for **commuting** and a **crossbody** **bag** when I **want** **hands** **free** on **busy** **streets**. **Bulky** **totes** **tip** me **off** **balance**; **sleek** **nylon** or **canvas** in **neutral** **colours** **last** **longer** than **trendy** **shapes** I'll **tire** of **next** **season**.

---
#### 3. Do you usually carry a bag when you go out?
**Almost** **always** unless I'm **literally** **stepping** **out** for **five** **minutes** with **phone** and **card** **only**. **Work** **days** mean **laptop** **sleeve**, **notebook**, and **water** **bottle**; **weekends** I **downsize** but **still** **pack** **sunglasses** and a **reusable** **cup** **habitually**.

---
#### 4. What do you put in your bag?
**Essentials**—**wallet**, **keys**, **phone**, **earphones**, sometimes **lip** **balm** and **painkillers** if I'm **travelling** **far**. **Study** **days** add **pens**, **printed** **notes**, and **snacks**; I **try** to **declutter** **weekly** because **receipts** and **old** **tickets** **multiply** **silently**.

---
#### 5. Have you ever lost a bag?
**Once** on a **crowded** **bus**—I **left** a **small** **gym** **bag** **under** the **seat** and **realised** **two** **stops** **later**; **nothing** **valuable**, but my **favourite** **hoodie** **vanished** with it. Since then I **do** a **quick** **pat-down** **check** before **standing** up, **especially** when I'm **tired**.

---
#### 6. Do you prefer backpacks or handbags?
**Backpacks** for **weight** **distribution** and **long** **walks**; **handbags** feel **awkward** on my **frame** unless they're **crossbody**. For **formal** **events** I might **carry** a **small** **crossbody** **instead**—**vanity** **versus** **comfort**, and **comfort** **usually** **wins**.
