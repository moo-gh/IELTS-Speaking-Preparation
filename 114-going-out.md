#### 1. Do you like going out?
**Yes**, **when** **energy** and **budget** **allow**—**cafés**, **films**, and **short** **walks** with **friends** **break** **routine** **nicely**. **Heavy** **study** **weeks** **push** me **indoors**, but **a** **quick** **outing** **usually** **lifts** my **mood**.

---

**A** **few** **times** **per** **week** for **casual** **plans**—**more** on **weekends**, **less** during **exam** **crunch**. **Daily** **errands** **count** as **going** **out** **too**, even if they're **not** **exciting**.

---
