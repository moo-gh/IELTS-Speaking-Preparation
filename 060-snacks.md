#### 1. What snacks do you like?
**Savoury** **over** **sweet** most days—**nuts**, **rice** **crackers**, or **hummus** with **carrot** **sticks** when I'm **studying**. If I'm **treating** myself, **dark** **chocolate** or **butter** **cookies** **hit** the **spot**, but I **try** not to **keep** a **whole** **tin** **within** **arm's** **reach**.

---
#### 2. Did you often eat snacks when you were young?
**Yes**—after **school** I'd **raid** the **kitchen** for **fruit**, **yoghurt**, or **whatever** **biscuits** were **allowed** before **dinner**. **Weekends** meant **crisps** during **cartoons**; my **parents** **limited** **sugary** **stuff** but **weren't** **strict** about **plain** **bread** and **cheese**.

---
#### 3. When do you usually eat snacks?
**Mid**-**afternoon** when **lunch** feels **far** **away** and my **focus** **slips**, or **late** **evening** if **dinner** was **early** and I'm **still** **up** **working**. I **avoid** **heavy** **snacking** **right** before **bed** because **sleep** gets **lighter** and I **wake** **thirsty**.

---
#### 4. Do you think it is healthy to eat snacks?
**It** **depends** on **what** and **how** **often**—**small** **portions** of **protein** or **fibre** can **bridge** **hunger** **without** **derailing** **meals**. **Constant** **ultra**-**processed** **bags** of **salt** and **sugar** are **hard** to **justify**; I **treat** those as **occasional**, not **daily**, **fuel**.

---
#### 5. Do people in your country like snacks?
**Very** **much**—**street** **vendors**, **bakeries**, and **supermarket** **aisles** are **packed** with **quick** **bites** for **commuters** and **students**. **Traditional** **sweets** and **salty** **pastries** **appear** at **gatherings**; **health** **trends** **push** **nuts** and **yoghurt** **too**, so **tastes** are **splitting** a **bit**.

---
#### 6. What is your favourite snack?
**Probably** **mixed** **nuts** with a **pinch** of **salt**—**crunchy**, **filling**, and **easy** to **portion** into a **small** **container**. **Second** place goes to **warm** **flatbread** **with** **herbs** when someone **bakes** at **home**; **shop**-**bought** **versions** rarely **match** that **smell**.
