#### 1. Do you enjoy giving gifts?
**Yes**, **when** I **know** the **person** **well** enough to **pick** something **useful** or **personal**—**not** **random** **luxury** for **show**. **Last**-**minute** **panic** **shopping** **stresses** me **out**; **planned** **gifts** with **a** **story** **feel** **much** **better**.

---
#### 2. What do you consider when choosing a gift?
**Their** **tastes**, **budget**, and **whether** they **already** **own** **too** **much** **clutter**. **Practical** **beats** **flashy** for **most** **friends**—**books**, **quality** **snacks**, or **small** **gear** they **mentioned** **once** in **chat**.

---
#### 3. Have you ever received a great gift?
**Yes**—a **relative** **once** **gave** me a **dictionary** **set** when I **started** **serious** **English** **study**; I **used** it **for** **years**. **Concert** **tickets** from **friends** **rank** **high** **too** because **memories** **outlast** **objects**.

---
#### 4. Do you think you are good at choosing gifts?
**Average**, **honestly**—I **nail** it for **close** **friends** who **drop** **hints**, but **wider** **family** **events** **leave** me **guessing**. I **ask** **siblings** for **backup** **ideas** rather than **risking** **awkward** **mismatches**.

---
#### 5. Have you ever given a handmade gift?
**A** **few** **times**—**homemade** **cards**, **simple** **photo** **albums**, and **once** a **badly** **decorated** **cake** that **friends** **politely** **praised**. **Handmade** **works** for **small** **circles**; **professional** **jobs** **deserve** **professional** **presents**.

---
#### 6. What kinds of gifts are common in your country?
**Sweets**, **flowers**, **perfume**, **clothing**, and **cash** in **envelopes** at **weddings** or **major** **celebrations**. **Parents** **often** **give** **meaningful** **items** at **milestones** like **graduation**; **gadgets** and **gift** **cards** **grow** **more** **common** among **younger** **people**.
