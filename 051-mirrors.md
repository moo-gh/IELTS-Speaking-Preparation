#### 1. Do you like looking at yourself in the mirror?
**Not** for **vanity**'s **sake**—I **glance** to **check** I'm **presentable** before **leaving** the **house**, or after **brushing** my **teeth** when the **light** is **good**. **Staring** for **too** **long** **feels** **odd**; I **treat** a **mirror** as a **practical** **tool**, not a **hobby** **in** **itself**.

---
#### 2. How often do you look in the mirror?
**Several** **times** a **day** in **passing**—**washing** my **face**, **styling** my **hair**, **adjusting** a **collar** in a **shop** **window** **counts** **too** in a **sense**. I don't **keep** a **compact** **mirror** in my **bag** unless I'm **travelling** and **might** need to **fix** **something** **quickly** **between** **meetings**.

---
#### 3. Have you ever bought a mirror for your home?
**Once**—a **simple** **full-length** **one** with a **slim** **frame** for the **hallway** so I could **see** an **outfit** **head-to-toe** before **heading** **out**. **Online** **shopping** was **risky** because **glass** can **arrive** **scratched**, so I **read** **reviews** and **picked** a **shop** with a **solid** **returns** **policy**.

---
#### 4. Are mirrors good decorations?
**They** **can** be—especially if a **room** is **dim** or **cramped**; **strategically** **placed** **glass** can **bounce** **light** and make **spaces** feel **larger**. I'm not **keen** on **gilt** **baroque** **styles** **everywhere**, but a **clean-lined** **round** **mirror** above a **console** **often** **looks** **balanced** **rather** than **showy**.

---
#### 5. Did you have a mirror in your room when you were a child?
**A** **small** **one** above the **sink** in the **shared** **bathroom** was **mainly** what I **used**; I didn't **have** a **giant** **freestanding** **mirror** in my **bedroom** **until** I was **older**. **Teenagers** **tend** to **care** more about **skin** and **hair**, so I **appreciated** a **decent** **shaving**-**height** **surface** **eventually** **when** we **moved** **flat**.

---
#### 6. Would you like to have mirrors on the walls at home in the future?
**A** **few** **well-chosen** **ones**, **yes**—perhaps in the **entryway** and **maybe** one in a **home** **gym** **corner** if I **ever** **set** that **up**, for **form** **checks** during **stretches**. I wouldn't **tile** **every** **wall** like a **dance** **studio**; **too** **many** **reflections** can feel **busy** and **distracting** when I'm **trying** to **relax** **after** **work**.
