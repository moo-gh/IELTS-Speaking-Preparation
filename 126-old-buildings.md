#### 1. Have you ever seen old buildings in the city?
**Yes**, **many**—**historic** **mosques**, **bazaar** **rows**, **brick** **houses** with **courtyards**, and **colonial**-**era** **facades** **mixed** into **busy** **districts**. **Tourist** **walks** **feel** **richer** when **new** **glass** **towers** **sit** **beside** **structures** **older** than **my** **grandparents**.

---
#### 2. Do you think we should preserve old buildings in cities?
**Yes**, **selectively**—**demolishing** **everything** **erases** **identity**, but **unsafe** **ruins** **need** **honest** **repair** or **careful** **replacement**. **Restoration** **with** **local** **materials** **keeps** **character** **alive** **better** than **plastic** **facades** **pretending** to be **historic**.

---
#### 3. Do you prefer living in an old building or a modern house?
**Modern** **for** **daily** **comfort**—**insulation**, **lifts**, and **reliable** **plumbing** **matter** when **winters** **turn** **harsh**. I'd **visit** **old** **buildings** **happily**, but **living** **there** **depends** on **how** **well** they're **maintained**.

---
#### 4. Are there any old buildings you want to see in the future?
**Several** **abroad**—**famous** **cathedrals**, **palaces**, and **ancient** **sites** I've **studied** in **history** **classes**. **Locally**, **restored** **caravanserais** and **old** **city** **gates** **stay** on my **list** for **proper** **guided** **visits**.

---
#### 5. Do you like taking photos of old buildings?
**Yes**, **especially** **textures**—**weathered** **brick**, **carved** **wood**, **domes** at **sunset**. **Crowds** and **bad** **light** **frustrate** me, but **one** **good** **shot** **rewards** **patience** **walking** **side** **streets**.

---
#### 6. What problems do old buildings face in cities today?
**Neglect**, **pollution**, **illegal** **extensions**, and **pressure** from **developers** who **prefer** **profitable** **towers**. **Earthquakes** and **poor** **maintenance** **turn** **heritage** into **danger** unless **governments** **fund** **real** **conservation**.
