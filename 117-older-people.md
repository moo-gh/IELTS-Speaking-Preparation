#### 1. Have you ever worked with older people?
**Yes**, **briefly**—**volunteer** **help** at a **community** **centre** and **group** **projects** where **older** **mentors** **guided** **younger** **students**. **Patience** and **clear** **communication** **mattered** **more** than **speed** when **explaining** **tech** or **forms**.

---
#### 2. Are you happy to work with people older than you?
**Generally**, **yes**—they **often** **bring** **experience**, **calm**, and **stories** that **put** **daily** **stress** in **perspective**. **Friction** **happens** when **methods** **clash**, but **respect** and **listening** **usually** **smooth** it **out**.

---
#### 3. What are the benefits of spending time with older people?
**Perspective**, **practical** **advice**, and **reminders** that **problems** **cycle**—**what** **feels** **huge** at **twenty** **often** **shrinks** **later**. **They** **also** **pass** down **family** **history** and **skills** like **cooking** or **repair** **work** **younger** **generations** **skip**.

---
#### 4. Do you enjoy spending time with older people?
**Yes**, **especially** **grandparents** and **older** **uncles** during **holidays**—**tea**, **stories**, and **gentle** **teasing** **feel** **warm**. **Long** **lectures** **without** **breaks** **tire** me, but **balanced** **visits** **leave** me **happier**.

---
#### 5. Do you often visit your grandparents or older relatives?
**Several** **times** a **year** for **major** **holidays**, plus **short** **calls** or **messages** when **busy** **weeks** **block** **travel**. **Distance** and **work** **limit** **frequency**, but **we** **try** to **show** **up** for **important** **family** **events**.

---
#### 6. What can young people learn from older people?
**Patience**, **frugality**, **relationship** **skills**, and **how** to **handle** **setbacks** **without** **drama**. **Tech** **tips** **flow** the **other** **way**, but **life** **judgment** **still** **leans** **heavily** toward **people** who've **seen** **more** **seasons**.
