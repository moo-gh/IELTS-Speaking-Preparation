#### 1. Do you like walking?
**Yes**—it's **low-impact** **exercise** I can **fit** into a **busy** day without **gear** or a **gym** **membership**. I especially enjoy **walking** when the **weather** is **mild** and I can **clear** my **head**, though I'm less **enthusiastic** about **power-walking** in **heavy** **rain** or **freezing** **wind**.

---
#### 2. Do you walk a lot?
**Reasonably**—I **aim** for **at** **least** a **decent** **stretch** **daily**, **whether** that's a **brisk** **commute** on **foot** or an **evening** **stroll** after **dinner**. On **sedentary** **workdays** I **deliberately** **take** **short** **breaks** to **stretch** my **legs** so I'm not **sitting** for **hours** **on** **end**.

---
#### 3. Where do you like to go for a walk?
**Parks** and **tree-lined** **paths** are my **favourite**—**greener** **spaces** feel **calmer** than **traffic** **noise**. **Occasionally** I'll **wander** around **historic** **quarters** in the **city** **centre** just to **people-watch** and **window-shop**, but I **prefer** **routes** where I can **hear** **birds** rather than **horns**.

---
#### 4. Do you prefer walking alone or with someone else?
**It depends**. **Solo** **walks** are **ideal** when I need **quiet** **thinking** time or I'm **listening** to a **podcast**. **Walking** with a **friend** or **family** **member** is **better** for **catching** **up** and **making** **exercise** feel **social** rather than a **chore**.

---
#### 5. Did you walk more as a child than you do now?
**Probably** yes—**school** was **within** **walking** **distance** for me, and **weekends** meant **playing** **outside** until it got **dark**. **Adult** **life** is more **car-** or **public-transport-** **dependent**, so I have to **plan** **walking** **on** **purpose** instead of it being **automatic**.

---
#### 6. Do you think walking in the countryside is better than walking in the city?
**Broadly**, **countryside** **walking** wins on **air** **quality**, **views**, and **peace**—**hiking** **trails** feel like a **proper** **escape**. **City** **walking** is still **valuable** for **convenience** and **exploring** **neighbourhoods**; it's just **noisier** and you have to **watch** for **pollution** and **busy** **junctions**.
