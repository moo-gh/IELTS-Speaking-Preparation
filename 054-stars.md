#### 1. Do you often look at the stars?
**Not** as a **daily** **ritual**—I **glance** **up** when I'm **walking** home on a **clear** **night** or **stepping** **outside** after **work**, but I'm **not** **lying** on a **blanket** with a **chart** **every** **weekend**. **Light** **pollution** in the **city** **washes** **out** a lot of **detail**, so the **view** **depends** **heavily** on **where** I **happen** to be.

---
#### 2. Is it easy to see stars where you live?
**In** **town**, **only** the **brightest** **pinpricks** **punch** **through**—**planets** can **look** like **steady** **dots** and you might **catch** **Orion**'s **belt** if you're **lucky**. **Trips** **out** to **hills** or **coast** on a **moonless** **evening** are a **different** **story**; then the **band** of the **Milky** **Way** **starts** to make **sense** **rather** than **feeling** like a **theory** in a **textbook**.

---
#### 3. Did you learn about stars at school?
**A** **bit** in **general** **science**—**names** of **planets**, **order** from the **Sun**, **why** **seasons** **shift**, and **how** **telescopes** **work** at a **basic** **level**. I **remember** **diagrams** more than **stargazing** **homework**; **practical** **astronomy** wasn't really on the **curriculum** **beyond** **odd** **planetarium** **trips** if your **school** could **afford** them.

---
#### 4. Are you interested in news about space?
**Casually** **yes**—**rover** **landings**, **new** **images** from **orbiting** **probes**, or **discoveries** about **distant** **exoplanets** are **fascinating** **headlines** over **breakfast**. I don't **follow** **every** **launch** **schedule** like a **die-hard** **fan**, but I **like** that **science** **teams** are **still** **pushing** **instruments** **further** than our **grandparents** could **imagine**.

---
#### 5. Would you like to travel to space if you had the chance?
**Hypothetically**, **once** the **risks** and **environmental** **costs** were **managed** **better** than they are for **billionaire** **stunts** today—I'd **love** a **short** **orbit** to **see** the **curve** of the **Earth** and the **thin** **blue** **shell** of **atmosphere**. **Realistically**, I'm **happy** on the **ground** for **now**; **training**, **g-forces**, and **motion** **sickness** **sound** **grim** enough to **keep** it on a **bucket** **list**, not a **Tuesday** **plan**.

---
#### 6. Can you name any constellations?
**A** **handful** from **memory**—**Orion**, the **Big** **Dipper**, **Cassiopeia**'s **W**-**shape** on a **chart** I've **seen** **repeated** often enough. I'm **slower** at **spotting** them **without** an **app** **overlay**, **especially** when **city** **lights** **eat** the **fainter** **stars** and the **pattern** **doesn't** **click** **instantly**.
