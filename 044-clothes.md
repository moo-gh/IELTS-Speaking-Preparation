#### 1. Do you enjoy shopping for clothes?
**On the whole**, yes—**provided** I'm not **rushed** and I **know** roughly what I'm **looking for**. I like **trying things on** when the **fit** matters, but **crowded** malls on **weekends** can feel **overwhelming**, so I **tend to** **browse** online first and then **pop into** a shop **if** I need to **check** the **fabric** or **size**.

---
#### 2. What kind of clothes do you usually wear?
**Day to day**, **casual** **layers**—**plain** **T-shirts**, **jeans** or **chinos**, and a **lightweight** **jacket** depending on the **season**. For work I **dress** a bit more **formally**: **neat** **trousers** and a **button-down** shirt, **nothing** too **flashy**, because I want to look **put together** without **standing out** for the wrong reasons.

---
#### 3. Do you wear different kinds of clothes now compared to when you were younger?
**Definitely**. As a teenager I **followed** **trends** more **blindly**—**branded** **hoodies**, **oversized** cuts, that sort of thing. These days I **prioritise** **comfort** and **versatility**: fewer **impulse** buys, more **basics** I can **mix and match**, and I'm more **willing** to **pay** for **quality** **footwear** because it **lasts** longer.

---
#### 4. Is fashion important to you?
**Moderately**. I don't **obsess** over **runway** looks, but I **care** about **clean**, **appropriate** **outfits** for **occasions**—**job** **interviews**, **weddings**, **family** **gatherings**. **Self-expression** matters, but for me it's **subtle**: **colour** **choices**, **fit**, and **grooming** rather than **chasing** every **micro-trend**.

---
#### 5. Do people in your country follow fashion trends?
**Some** do **avidly**, especially in **big** **cities** where **influencers** and **fast** **fashion** **chains** are **everywhere**. **Others** are more **practical** and **budget-conscious**, **repeating** **wardrobe** **staples** for years. **Social** **media** has **speeded up** how **styles** **spread**, but not everyone has the **time** or **money** to **refresh** their look **constantly**.

---
#### 6. Have you ever bought second-hand clothes?
**Occasionally**—**vintage** **jackets** or **designer** pieces in **good** **condition** can be a **bargain**, and it's **more** **sustainable** than **buying** **brand-new** **items** you'll **barely** wear. I **draw the line** at **underwear** and **well-worn** **trainers** for **hygiene** reasons, but for **outerwear** I'm **open** to it **if** it **doesn't** look **threadbare**.
