#### 1. Do you enjoy looking at scenery?
**Yes**—**wide** **views** **slow** my **breathing** **faster** than **most** **indoor** **breaks**. **City** **blocks** **blur** **together** **daily**; **hills**, **coastlines**, or **even** **sunset** over **rooftops** **feel** like a **small** **reset**.

---
#### 2. Do you look at the scenery when travelling by bus or car?
**On** **longer** **trips**, **definitely**—I **pick** **window** **seats** and **watch** **fields**, **mountains**, or **road** **cuts** **change** **colour** with **season**. **Short** **commutes** I **mostly** **stare** at **phones**; **guilty** **admission**, but **traffic** **jams** **offer** **little** **beauty** **anyway**.

---
#### 3. Do you prefer mountains or the sea?
**Mountains** **slightly**—**cooler** **air**, **hiking** **trails**, and **layered** **ridges** **suit** me **better** than **humid** **beaches** in **peak** **summer**. I **still** **love** **the** **sea** for **short** **visits**; **living** **beside** it **might** **wear** **thin** for me **over** **time**.

---
#### 4. Do you like taking pictures of scenery?
**Sometimes**—**sunset** **lines**, **snow** on **distant** **peaks**, or **mist** in **valleys** when **light** **looks** **unrepeatable**. I **don't** **chase** **perfect** **shots**; **one** **quick** **photo** **beats** **missing** the **moment** while **fiddling** with **settings**.

---
#### 5. What is the most beautiful scenery you have seen?
**A** **mountain** **pass** after **spring** **rain**—**green** **slopes**, **clouds** **below** the **summit**, and **thin** **waterfalls** on **rock** **faces**. **Southern** **coast** **sunsets** **rank** **close** **second**; **orange** **sky** over **calm** **water** **stays** **vivid** **years** **later**.

---
#### 6. Is there nice scenery near where you live?
**Within** **reach**, **yes**—**nearby** **hills** for **weekend** **walks**, **parks** with **trees**, and **clear** **evening** **light** over **the** **city** **skyline**. **Truly** **wild** **landscapes** need a **few** **hours'** **drive**, but **locals** **treat** **those** **trips** as **normal** **weekend** **escapes**.
