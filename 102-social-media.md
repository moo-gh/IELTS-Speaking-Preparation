#### 1. Have you ever posted anything on social media?
**Yes**, **regularly**—**photos** from **trips**, **short** **updates** about **study** **milestones**, and **occasional** **memes** or **articles** I **find** **useful**. I **avoid** **posting** when **angry** or **tired** because **permanent** **feeds** **remember** **bad** **decisions**.

---
#### 2. When did you start using social media?
**Around** **secondary** **school**—**mostly** to **chat** with **classmates** and **share** **music** **links**. **Parents** were **cautious** at **first**; **rules** **relaxed** **gradually** once **homework** and **sleep** **didn't** **collapse**.

---
#### 3. Do you think you spend too much time on social media?
**Sometimes**, **especially** during **exam** **breaks** when **short** **scrolls** **become** **forty** **minutes**. I **use** **timers** and **delete** **apps** **temporarily** when **deadlines** **loom**; **balance** **returns** **faster** than **willpower** **alone**.

---
#### 4. Do your friends use social media?
**Almost** **everyone**—**group** **chats**, **event** **invites**, and **meme** **threads** **run** **daily** **life** **now**. **A** **few** **friends** **post** **rarely** but **still** **read** **messages**; **nobody** **fully** **offline** unless **travelling** **remote**.

---
#### 5. What do people often do on social media?
**Chat**, **share** **photos**, **watch** **short** **videos**, **follow** **news**, **promote** **small** **businesses**, and **debate** **politics** **too** **heatedly** at **times**. **Younger** **users** **chase** **trends**; **older** **relatives** **prefer** **family** **updates** and **voice** **notes**.

---
#### 6. What are the disadvantages of social media?
**Addiction**, **comparison** **anxiety**, **misinformation**, and **arguments** that **escalate** **fast** without **face**-**to**-**face** **tone**. **Privacy** **leaks** and **scams** **worry** me **too**—**thinking** **twice** before **clicking** **strange** **links** **saves** **headaches**.
