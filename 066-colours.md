#### 1. What's your favourite colour?
**Deep** **blue**—it **feels** **calm** without **looking** **dull**, and it **pairs** **well** with **grey** or **white** in **clothes** and **stationery**. I **avoid** **neon** **shades** for **everyday** **wear**; they **feel** **loud** on **me** even if I **like** them on **posters**.

---
#### 2. What's the most popular colour in your country?
**Hard** to **pin** **down**—**white** and **cream** **dominate** **traditional** **architecture** and **ceremonial** **clothing**, while **city** **fashion** **cycles** through **black**, **beige**, and **occasional** **bold** **accents**. **Cars** and **phones** **trend** **grey** and **silver** because they **resell** **well**, **apparently**.

---
#### 3. Do colours affect your mood?
**A** **bit**—**bright** **yellow** **kitchens** **cheer** me **up** **briefly**, and **dim** **brown** **rooms** can **feel** **heavy** if there's **not** enough **light**. **Clothing** **colour** **matters** **less** than **sleep** and **stress**, but I **notice** I **dress** **darker** when I'm **tired**.

---
#### 4. Do you prefer light or dark colours?
**Light** for **summer** and **small** **spaces**—they **reflect** **sun** and **make** **flats** **feel** **bigger**. **Dark** **navy** or **charcoal** for **winter** **coats** because **stains** **hide** **better** on **commutes**; I **mix** **both** rather than **committing** to **one** **palette**.

---
#### 5. Did you have a favourite colour when you were young?
**Yes**—I **phased** through **bright** **red**, then **green**, then **obsessed** over **anything** **metallic** on **stickers** and **pencil** **cases**. **Parents** **bought** **bedding** in those **colours** until I **suddenly** **declared** I'd **outgrown** them **overnight**.

---
#### 6. Are there any colours you dislike?
**Murky** **yellow**-**green** and **certain** **brown**-**orange** **combos** **feel** **dated** to me, like **old** **carpets** in **waiting** **rooms**. I **don't** **hate** them **morally**—I **just** **wouldn't** **choose** them for **walls** I **stare** at **daily**.
