#### 1. Do you like plants?
**Yes**, **especially** **green** **leaves** that **soften** **rooms** and **balconies** **without** **demanding** **constant** **attention**. I'm **not** a **botanist**; I **enjoy** **plants** as **calm** **background** **life** rather than a **serious** **hobby**.

**Yes**, **often**—**potted** **flowers**, **small** **trees**, or **herb** **sets** for **housewarmings**, **offices**, and **spring** **visits**. **Living** **gifts** **symbolise** **growth** and **blessing**; **recipients** **appreciate** them if they **actually** **have** **light** and **time** to **care**.
