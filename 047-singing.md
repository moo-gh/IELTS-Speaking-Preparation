#### 1. Do you like singing?
**Yes**, but **mostly** in **private** settings—**in** the **car**, **while** **cooking**, or **humming** along to **headphones**. I'm not **confident** enough to **belt** out **ballads** in front of **strangers**, but I **enjoy** the **feeling** of **matching** a **melody** when nobody's **judging** my **pitch**.

---
#### 2. Can you sing well?
I'd say I'm **average**—I can **stay** **roughly** **in** **tune** on **simple** **choruses**, but I'm **not** **naturally** **gifted** with a **powerful** **range** or **perfect** **timing**. **Karaoke** is **fun** for me as a **social** **activity**, not because I **expect** **applause** for **technical** **skill**.

---
#### 3. When do you usually sing?
**Most** **often** when I'm **alone** and **doing** **mundane** **tasks**—**cleaning**, **commuting**, **walking**—because **music** makes the time **pass** **faster**. **Occasionally** I'll **join** in at **birthday** **parties** when everyone else is **singing** **too** **loudly** for anyone to **notice** **mistakes**.

---
#### 4. Do you want to be a singer?
**Not** as a **career**—I **admire** **professional** **vocalists**, but I don't have the **drive** to **train** **daily** or **perform** **under** **spotlights**. I'd **rather** **keep** singing as a **hobby** that **lifts** my **mood** without **turning** it into **work** **pressure**.

---
#### 5. Will you take singing lessons in the future?
**Maybe** **briefly**, **out** of **curiosity**—I'd **like** to **learn** **breathing** **techniques** and **warm-ups** so I **strain** my **voice** less. **That** said, I'm not **planning** **long-term** **coaching** unless I **suddenly** **commit** to a **choir** or a **serious** **project**.

---
#### 6. Did you enjoy singing when you were younger?
**Yes**—**school** **assemblies** and **music** **classes** were **low-stakes** ways to **sing** **together**, and I **liked** **chanting** **rhymes** on **trips**. I was **never** the **lead** in a **show**, but **group** **singing** felt **less** **embarrassing** than **solo** **performances** do **now**.
