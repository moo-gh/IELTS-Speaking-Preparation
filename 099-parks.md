#### 1. Did you like going to parks as a child?
**Yes**, **very** **much**—**running** on **grass**, **climbing** **play** **equipment**, and **eating** **snacks** **parents** **brought** from **home**. **Weekend** **park** **trips** **felt** **special** because they **broke** the **routine** of **school** and **homework** **without** **needing** a **long** **holiday**.

---
#### 2. Do you still like going to parks now?
**Yes**, but **differently**—**less** **playground** **chaos**, **more** **walking**, **reading** on **benches**, and **meeting** **friends** for **coffee** **nearby**. **Green** **space** **clears** my **head** after **busy** **weeks** **stuck** **indoors** over **screens**.

---
#### 3. Would you like to see more parks in your city?
**Definitely**—**new** **towers** **sprout** **faster** than **trees** in **some** **districts**, and **families** **need** **safe** **places** to **exercise** and **relax**. **Small** **pocket** **parks** near **dense** **neighbourhoods** **matter** as **much** as **huge** **central** **gardens**.

---
#### 4. Are there any parks you want to go to in the future?
**Several** **abroad**—**famous** **royal** **parks** and **botanical** **gardens** I've **seen** **only** in **documentaries**. **Locally**, a **restored** **riverside** **walk** and a **hill** **park** with **city** **views** **stay** on my **list** for **spring** **weather**.

---
#### 5. What do people usually do in parks in your country?
**Picnics**, **football**, **walking**, **children** on **bikes**, **elderly** **groups** **chatting** under **trees**, and **families** **flying** **kites** on **windy** **days**. **In** **spring**, **parks** **fill** with **photographers** and **couples** **enjoying** **milder** **evenings**.

---
#### 6. How often do you visit parks?
**Every** **few** **weeks** in **good** **weather**—**more** in **spring** and **autumn**, **less** in **summer** **heat** or **winter** **rain**. If **exams** **pile** **up**, **visits** **drop** **sharply**, but I **try** to **return** **once** **pressure** **eases**.
