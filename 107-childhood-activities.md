#### 1. What are your favourite activities?
**Reading**, **walking**, **occasional** **gym** **sessions**, and **meeting** **friends** for **coffee** or **films** when **schedules** **align**. **Exam** **periods** **shrink** the **list**, but I **try** to **keep** **one** **low**-**pressure** **habit** so **weeks** **don't** **feel** **only** **about** **deadlines**.

---
#### 2. What were your favourite activities when you were a child?
**Football** in **alleys**, **cycling** **near** **home**, **collecting** **cards**, and **building** **things** from **scrap** **materials** **we** **found**. **Rainy** **days** **meant** **board** **games**, **comics**, and **arguing** over **which** **cartoon** to **watch**.

---
#### 3. Did you prefer to do activities alone or with a group of people when you were a child?
**With** **friends**, **mostly**—**games** **felt** **pointless** **without** **someone** to **cheer** or **complain** **alongside** you. **Reading** and **drawing** were **exceptions** where **quiet** **solo** **time** **actually** **felt** **peaceful**.

---
#### 4. Are there any differences between the activities you liked when you were a child and those you like now?
**Huge** **differences**—**childhood** was **loud**, **physical**, and **carefree**; **now** I **value** **calmer** **things** like **long** **walks**, **podcasts**, and **structured** **study**. I **still** **enjoy** **sport**, but **recovery** **time** **matters** **more** than it **did** at **ten**.

---
#### 5. What outdoor activities did you do as a child?
**Tag**, **hide**-**and**-**seek**, **football**, **kite** **flying** on **windy** **days**, and **short** **trips** to **parks** with **parents** on **weekends**. **Summer** **evenings** **stretched** **forever** because **nobody** **checked** **phones** **every** **five** **minutes**.

---
#### 6. Do you think children today have different activities from when you were young?
**Yes**—**screens**, **online** **games**, and **structured** **classes** **fill** **more** **hours** **now**, while **free** **street** **play** **shrinks** in **big** **cities**. **Some** **changes** **help** **learning**; **others** **worry** me when **kids** **rarely** **go** **outside** **without** **planning**.
