#### 1. Is it difficult for you to stay focused?
**Sometimes**—my **attention** is **fine** when the **task** is **clear** and **deadlines** are **real**, but **vague** **work** or **long** **passages** of **reading** can **fracture** it. **Mood** and **sleep** **matter** **more** than I **admit**; **one** **bad** **night** and I **catch** myself **rereading** the **same** **line**.

---
#### 2. When do you need to concentrate?
**During** **IELTS** **practice**, **essay** **planning**, and **any** **meeting** where I **take** **notes** that **must** be **accurate**. **Commuting** is **low**-**stakes**; **exam** **simulations** and **grammar** **drills** are where I **need** **deep** **focus** **without** **tab**-**switching**.

---
#### 3. What do you do to improve your concentration?
**Time** **blocks**, **phone** in **another** **room**, and **short** **breaks** on a **timer** so I **don't** **burn** **out**. **Background** **instrumental** **music** **helps** **sometimes**; **silence** **works** **better** for **listening** **tasks** where **every** **syllable** **counts**.

---
#### 4. What may distract you when you are trying to concentrate?
**Notifications**, **noisy** **neighbours**, **hunger**, and **worry** about **unfinished** **errands**—**mental** **noise** can be **worse** than **physical** **noise**. **Open** **browser** **tabs** are my **worst** **habit**; I **close** them **aggressively** before **serious** **sessions**.

---
#### 5. Did you stay focused in class as a child?
**Mixed**—I **paid** **attention** in **subjects** I **liked**, but **daydreamed** in **lessons** that felt **slow** or **repetitive**. **Teachers** who **asked** **questions** **kept** me **sharp**; **long** **lectures** without **interaction** **lost** me **by** the **middle** of the **period**.

---
#### 6. Is it easy to concentrate in a noisy environment?
**Rarely** for **language** **study**—I **need** **predictable** **sound** or **quiet**. **Mild** **café** **hum** can **work** for **light** **admin**, but **sirens**, **drilling**, or **loud** **conversations** **next** to me **shatter** **focus** **within** **minutes**.
