#### 1. Do you like visiting museums?
**Generally** **yes**, **when** I'm **not** **rushed**—I **like** **slow** **looking** and **reading** **short** **captions** rather than **power**-**walking** past **glass** **cases**. **Overpacked** **itineraries** **ruin** it; **one** **focused** **gallery** **beats** **five** **floors** **ticked** off a **list**.

---
#### 2. How often do you visit museums?
**A** **few** **times** a **year**, **usually** when **friends** **visit** or a **special** **exhibition** **opens** **locally**. **Between** **work** and **study** I **don't** **go** **spontaneously**, but I **try** to **book** **tickets** **ahead** for **anything** **time**-**limited**.

---
#### 3. What kind of museums do you like?
**History** and **everyday** **life** **museums** **interest** me **most**—**tools**, **clothes**, and **maps** that **show** how **ordinary** **people** **lived**. **Modern** **art** is **hit**-**or**-**miss** for me; I **enjoy** **clever** **installations** but **tune** **out** when a **label** **feels** **pretentious**.

---
#### 4. When was the last time you visited a museum?
**Last** **autumn**—a **temporary** **photo** **exhibition** about **urban** **change** in my **city**. I **spent** **about** **ninety** **minutes** there, **then** **sat** in the **café** **debating** which **neighbourhood** **photos** felt **honest** versus **staged**.

---
#### 5. Do you prefer visiting museums alone or with others?
**Alone** for **concentration**; **with** **friends** when I **want** **conversation** **afterwards**. **Solo** visits let me **linger**; **group** visits **mean** **compromise** on **pace**, but **shared** **reactions** can **make** **boring** **rooms** **funny**.

---
#### 6. Are there many museums in your hometown?
**Several** **worth** **seeing**—a **regional** **history** **museum**, a **small** **art** **gallery**, and **occasional** **pop**-**up** **spaces** in **converted** **warehouses**. **Tourism** **isn't** **massive**, so **crowds** stay **manageable** **except** during **school** **holidays**.
